 What should I bring?

It is very important to be well prepared, as weather conditions can change quickly. The ecosystems that will be visited are characterized by rain, sun, strong winds. therefore it is essential to have the necessary implements or basic personal clothing:


  •   Hiking or sport boots with good grip.
  •   Comfortable pants for walking
  •   Gloves
  •  Sun hat
  •  Sunscreen
  •  Change T-shirt
  •   Sunglasses with UV filter
 Important note.

San Gil is an exciting destination with a variety of outdoor activities, but it is essential to be informed and prepared before your visit. :


  San Gil is at a significant altitude, which can take a toll on your body. Be prepared for changes in atmospheric pressure, variable temperatures and lower oxygen levels compared to coastal areas.

  It is recommended that you be in acceptable physical condition, as many of the activities in San Gil involve outdoor adventures that can be physically demanding. If you have medical concerns, consult a professional before participating.

  Be sure to bring your identification document or passport with you, as it may be required for check-in at some tourist locations and activities.
