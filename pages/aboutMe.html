About us

Inu tour was created at the beginning of the year 2023 in order to promote preservation by taking you to unique places created by nature.

Our logo is inspired by the muisca and chibcha culture, the first name INU in the muisca language represents luggage and provisions and in the chincha language food for the one who comes on the road.
