El Cocuy-Natural Adventure

Cocuy is a mountainous paradise of snow-capped peaks, crystalline lagoons and a unique biodiversity. Our tour agency takes you to explore this natural wonder on unforgettable excursions.


 ¿What does the plan include?


  •   Private transportation to and from Bogota.
  •   Accompanying tour guide.
  •  Meals.
  •  Hotel accommodations.
  •  Internal transportation.
  •  Travel and mountain insurance.
  •  Acclimatization hike.
  •  Registration and entrance to El Cocuy National Natural Park.
  •  Trekking and hiking in the Sierra Nevada de El Cocuy.
  •  Local guides endorsed by National Parks of Colombia (certified in first aid and mountain rescue).
  •  Natural hot springs.
  •  Accident insurance.

 What should I bring?

Weather conditions can vary rapidly. The ecosystems to be visited are characterized by high temperatures, low temperatures, rain, sun, strong winds... therefore it is essential to have the necessary implements or basic personal clothing:


  •   Cold weather jacket or waterproof jacket.
  •   Hiking or sports boots with good grip, non-slip soles.
  •  Comfortable walking pants.
  •  Cold weather hat.
  •  Gloves
  •  Sun hat.
  •   Sunscreen.
  •  Waterproof or windproof coat.
  •  Change shirt.
  •  Sunglasses with UV filter.
  •  A whistle or whistlestick.
  •  Water bottle or water bottle.
  •  Change socks.
  •  Bathing suit.
  •  Lighter or matches.
  •  Garbage bag.
  •  Personal documents.
