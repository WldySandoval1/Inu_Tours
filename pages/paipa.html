 What should I bring?

It’s important to consider Paipa´s weather, that’s why you should take comfort clothes in case of weather changes.


  •   Cold weather jacket.
  •   Sunscreen.
  •  Swimming suit.
  •  Personal ID.
 Recommendations:

  Hydration: Keep hydrated during the trip. Take a bottle of water with you and make sure you drink enough water, especially if you´re spending time in the hot springs, where the water temperature can be high.

  Keep you save: If you want to be part of the water activities in the yacht club, follow the instructions and make sure to use every safety equipment provided. If you don’t know how to swim, you must inform the staff so they can take the appropriate actions.

  Chill out in the hot springs: If you visit this place, try to relax, and enjoy every remedial benefit.

  Respect for the natural environment: Remember, you are visiting a natural enviroment. Respect the local flora and fauna and don’t litter. Enjoy the view of the nature beauty in the most responsible way.
